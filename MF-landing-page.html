Connecting Landlords & Tentants

Rent Connect allows property owners and tenants find each other in a more efficient and low risk way. Vacancies are matched with the right tenants through desired amenities.

Tenants can also add in various documentation that assists in vetting them as amazing tenants, streamiling and fast-tracking the renatal application.

HOW IT WORKS

  • Create a Profile

    You create a profile specifing if your looking to rent, or looking to fill a vacancy

  • Post

    Property owners will input their vacancy unit to match with tenants who have inputed their preffered amenitites

  • Match

    Now that the match has occured, a more detail conversation to move forward can take place!
