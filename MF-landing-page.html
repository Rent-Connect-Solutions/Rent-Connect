WHAT IS RENT CONNECT?

Rent Connect is a app built by Rent Connect Solutions. We believe in alternative ideas and forward thinking to what we build and firmly believe that the rental real estate market can find value and purpose with Rent Connect app.

OUR VISION

Our vision is simple at its core and its to build a application that connects people, while they interact with the rental real estate industry. Building a new angle to a very complex industry while generating value for all parties involved.

HOW IT WORKS

  • Create a Profile

    Property owners and tenants can create a profile and personlize it the way you want

  • Submit

    Property owners will input their vacancy to match with tenants inputing their post to match

  • Match

    Now that the match has occured, a more detail conversation to move forward can take place!
