Our Story

Once upon a time in Paris…

It all started on a trip Europe, where I was planning on proposing to my girlfriend (thankfully she said yes). While browsing some local newspapers, I stumbled upon a section where people were posting about themselves looking for places to rent.

I started thinking, what if there was a platform that allowed tenants to advertise and list their wants and needs and match with available rental properties. This would reduce the endless scrolling for rentals by tenants, while simultaneously allowing property owners to fill their vacancies in a faster and more efficient way.
