Our Story

Once upon a time in Paris…

It all started on a trip to Europe, where I was planning on proposing to my girlfriend (thankfully she said yes). While browsing some local newspapers, I stumbled upon a section where people were posting about themselves looking for places to rent.

I started thinking, what if there was a platform that allowed tenants to advertise their rental wants & needs and include all the documentation required for vetting purposes. This could allow anyone to match with a vacant rental property, where ever they're posted geographically. Reducing doom scrolling for sought after rental units by tenants, while simultaneously allowing landlords and property managers to fill their vacancies in a faster and more efficient way.
