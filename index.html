Connecting Landlords & Tentants

Rent Connect allows property owners and tenants to find each other in a more efficient, low risk way. Vacancies are matched with the right tenants through desired amenities.

Tenants can also add in various documentation that assists in vetting them as amazing tenants, streamiling & fast-tracking the renatal application.

HOW IT WORKS

A new approach to helping property owners fill vancacies simultaneously helping tenants find their new home!

  • Create a Profile

    You create a profile specifing if you're looking to rent, or looking to fill a vacancy

  • Post

    Property owners will input their vacancy unit to match with tenants preffered amenitites

  • Match

    Now that the match has occured, a more detail conversation to move forward can take place!
