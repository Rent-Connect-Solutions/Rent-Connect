Connecting Landlords & Tentants

Our app is designed to connect property owners and tenants in a smarter, more efficient way. Property owners can actively search for pre-vetted tenants based on their preferences, streamlining the rental process and minimizing vacancies. Tenants can boost their profiles with key documents, making it easier to secure the right home faster.

HOW IT WORKS


With each post made, Rent Connect dynamically matchs all the criteria of your vacancy to a tenant matching the same criteria or as close of a match as possible.
The more detailed the posts (including vetting documentation,references,reports, etc.) will result in a better match while simultaneously assiting in vetting.
